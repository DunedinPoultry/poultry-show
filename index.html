About the Show

The Southern Poultry Show is a celebration of New Zealand's finest poultry, pigeons, ducks, bantams, cage birds, and more. Join breeders, exhibitors, and families from across the country for an exciting weekend of competition, community, and feathers!

Our 2026 show is proudly sponsored by Topflite and features special events, youth classes, and the Monty Wright Memorial Cage Bird Show.

Show Weekend Schedule

  • 📦 Friday May 22: Penning from 2:00 pm – 9:00 pm
  • 🕗 Saturday May 23: Judging from 8:30 am • Public viewing from 12:00–5:00 pm
  • 🥇 Youth Showmanship at 1:30 pm • Prizegiving Dinner at 6:00 pm
  • 🕙 Sunday May 24: Public viewing from 10:00 am – 2:00 pm • De-benching at 2:00 pm
