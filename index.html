About the Show

Join us for the Southern Poultry Show 2025 – a celebration of passion, feathers, and fine breeding! Open to all exhibitors across Aotearoa, with categories for poultry, ducks, bantams, pigeons, cage birds and more.

📍 *Dunedin Showgrounds*
📅 *September 20–21, 2025*

Event Schedule

  • 🗓 Friday, Sep 19 – Penning & Setup (4pm–8pm)
  • 🗓 Saturday, Sep 20 – Judging (9am start), Public Viewing (12–5pm)
  • 🗓 Sunday, Sep 21 – Public Viewing (10am–3pm), Prizegiving (2pm), Pack Down
